About

I am a self-taught passionate makeup artist who believes that makeup is art and beauty is spirit. I am a girl who persues her dreams. This is what I love doing and a part of who I am.

I have been working professionally in the makeup industry for over 5 years. I specialize in makeup for brides, special occasions, celebrities, fashion shows, tv shows, theatre and more. I have worked with celebrities like Georgina Onuoha, Miss Larnaca Pageant Show 2017, Theatre Skala and top brands such as M.A.C Cosmetics, L'Oreal Paris and NARS.
